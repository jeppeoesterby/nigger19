Auto-tune assistant

Asks Claude Sonnet to analyze all feedback entries and propose concrete prompt edits. Nothing is auto-applied — you see a diff and approve each change individually.

Built-in safeguards

  • Min. 3 supporting cases: every suggestion must cite at least 3 specific feedback entries. Patterns with fewer cases are rejected and shown under "considered but rejected".
  • Verbatim diff: every change is an exact find/replace. If the model tries to suggest a vague "sharpen the prompt", the suggestion is rejected.
  • Confidence score: high (5+ cases), medium (3-4), low (3 borderline cases).
  • Versioned: every accepted change is saved as a new version. Roll back in one click.
  • Hallucination check: if the model cites feedback IDs that don't exist, the suggestion is rejected.
